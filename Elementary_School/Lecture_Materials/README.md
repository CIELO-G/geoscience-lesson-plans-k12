# Lecture Materials

This folder contains the lecture materials developed for the **Elementary School geoscience lesson plans** in this repository.

The materials include **PowerPoint presentations (.pptx)** and **PDF documents (.pdf)** designed to support classroom instruction based on the corresponding lesson plans.

These lecture materials provide:
- Visual explanations of geoscience concepts
- Classroom presentation slides for instructors
- Supporting diagrams, figures, and examples
- Structured content aligned with each lesson plan

Each file corresponds to a specific lesson and is intended to help teachers deliver the activities and scientific concepts described in the lesson plan documents.

The lecture materials are designed for **4th–5th grade students** and cover a range of topics in geoscience including:

- Introduction to Geoscience  
- The Water Cycle  
- Water Resources in Desert Environments  
- Water Purification  
- Layers of the Earth  
- Weathering and Erosion  
- Weather and Climate  
- Volcanoes  
- Natural Indicators in Environmental Chemistry  

These resources were developed as part of the **CIELO-G Geoscience Education and Transformation initiative**, supporting hands-on learning and geoscience literacy in K–12 education.
